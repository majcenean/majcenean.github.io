This poster brings to light the challenges of food insecurity and hunger among college students.

During my freshman year of university, this was a topic which was heartfelt to me as I felt it was an invisible issue amongst the student body, often experienced but never spoken about. Like many, coming from public school, I was apalled by the high prices of required textbooks. This led to the juxtaposition of a stack of books with an empty fridge - a cold, unforgiving atmosphere which stares back at the viewer without mercy.

Maj Jenkins 2021.
