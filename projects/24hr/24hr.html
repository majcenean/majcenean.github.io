open 24hr

Spring 2020. | Video Art, Glitch Art.

concept

A video art piece critiquing human effects of the capitalist machine. In exchange for the convenience of constant service, time is claimed from countless lives spent toiling across the globe in service to the consumer. As an effect of this system's prevalence in our lives, time begins to consume itself, the lines between day and night blur, and the natural world faces corruption with no return. As people, we have become "open 24hr, 7 days a week."
