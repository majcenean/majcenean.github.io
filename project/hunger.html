Hunger No More

Fall 2019. | Ad Campaign.

This poster brings to light the challenges of food insecurity and hunger among college students.

This is a topic that I felt was an invisible issue amongst the student body, often experienced but never spoken about. Like many, coming from public school, I was apalled by the high prices of required textbooks. This led to the juxtaposition of a stack of books with an empty fridge - a cold, unforgiving atmosphere which stares back at the viewer without mercy.
