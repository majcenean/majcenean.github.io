As a firm believer in a just and ethical world, Maj (they/them) is a San Francisco based graphic designer and conceptual artist passionate about envisioning better futures.

Inspired by the natural world which surrounds them and motivated to change the social issues they encounter, Maj values the role which visual art has to play in changing societal paradigms. They believe in design as a pure form of communication, using artistic practice to dissect dominant attitudes, analyze cultural narratives, and provide socio-political critique. Their work is rhetoric, statement, and message.
