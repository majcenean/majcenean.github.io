About

Maj is a graphic designer born, raised, and living in San Francisco, CA. They are a senior design major and classical studies minor at the University of San Francisco. They are proud to represent their Chinese-American heritage and nonbinary community.


I believe a just and ethical world is possible and art is a pathway to achieve it. We have the power to design a beautiful future.

Art is a human reflex; it transmits human knowledge, develops empathy, and allows us to connect to one another in ways not possible through speech and typical manners of communication; it is the communication of raw emotion.

I believe in telling stories and narratives through artwork; stories have the power to change our world. I think deeply about what stories we surround ourselves with, recurring themes and messages.

Artwork can say what everyone is afraid to say. I use my artwork to express myself and critique systems that otherwise go unquestioned.
