streetlamp

Spring 2021. | Open-World Narrative Web Game.

concept

This is an interactive web game which satirizes Orientalist tendencies in physical and fictional environments.

By exploring anti-Asian racism within visual and storytelling mediums that bleeds into the real world, it calls attention to the fetishization and sexualization Asian women suffer, in addition to criticizing racist tropes and stereotypes which are commonplace in Western media.
