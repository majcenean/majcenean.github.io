These are a few print materials which I created during my internship there through Spotlight on the Arts, a program by California Lawyers for the Arts.

During the Summer of 2017, I interned at Root Division, a San Francisco nonprofit that, amongst other visual arts and exhibition programming, provides educational art classes to youth and adults. These pieces advertised their programs for youth near Civic Center.
