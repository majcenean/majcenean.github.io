stygga castle

Fall 2021. | Illustration, costume design, stage-play adaptation.

concept

A stage adaptation of the ninth chapter of the 1999 fantasy novel Lady of the Lake (Original Polish: Pani Jeziora) by Andrzej Sapkowski (trans. David French official UK English).

adaptation

The adaptation involved carefully analyzing the chapter and dividing it into separate sections, then adjusting the narrative flow of the writing based upon setting in order to plan for physical stage direction. Special effects such as flight and teleportation were also taken into consideration.

costume

Attention to theme, character analysis, and references of historical costume were taken in order to develop original and unique looks for each protagonist in the scene. Separate moodboards were created for each character to develop their costumes.

text

All rights of The Witcher series and the Lady of the Lake text go to Andrzej Sapkowski and translator David French.
