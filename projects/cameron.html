Non-profit organization Cameron House needed a slideshow which depicted their rich history, ongoing programs, and upcoming events. This slideshow was created with the goal of being accessible to a variety of audiences including children and elders.

Considerations of scale and diverse audiences were taken. Readers' heights and language skills may vary. All slides are bilingual in English and Chinese.
