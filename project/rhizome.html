Rhizome

Spring 2021. | Choose Your Own Path Web Game.


This is an interactive web game which takes place in a future time where loneliness, once conceived of by the public as a simple unfortunate nuisance, is now the silent killer of many through deterioration of mental health.

The player faces ethical and economic dilemmas as the brains behind the operation of humanity's last hope - The Interface, a strange new technology of a global network of human minds, made possible by combining the capabilities of the mind with that of vast underground fungal networks.
