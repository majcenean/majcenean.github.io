Context

The Great Animal Orchestra ↗ was an immersive audiovisual art experience created by Bernie Krause, United Visual Artists, and Fondation Cartier pour l’art contemporain that explores the natural world through sound. The West Coast premiere of this show took place at the Exploratorium in Summer 2023. The role of the 2D team was to amplify this experience with wall graphics and signage.

Role

For the gallery walls, I reproduced infographics featured in the Great Animal Orchestra book, including accurate depictions of statistics in graphs and charts. Additionally, I created exhibit labels, wayfinding signage, and signage for auxilliary content.

Team

2D Design Team Lead: Henni Yama. 2D Designers: Lutzka Zivny, Keith Song, Maj Jenkins. Exhibit Developer: Gaia Liu.


Auxilliary signage, such as this lolly, invite people to use their personal devices to interact with the content.

I created over 20 add-on labels for the exhibit floor, which would be posted alongside existing exhibit labels.

The finished exhibit combines multiple labels to create an interactive station for social listening.
