Leo T. McCarthy Center Newsletters

Spring 2019 - Spring 2022. | Print ephemera.

context

During my time at the Leo T. McCarthy Center for Public Service and the Common Good as design intern, one of my main roles was the spearheading of the design and distribution of print newsletters. I worked together with Leslie Lombre, my supervisor, and Priana Aquino, copywriter intern, to create monthly newsletters. I created the current newsletter template in Adobe InDesign, which is currently still in use by the office.

takeaways

This ongoing project allowed me to understand the process of content selection and refinement in a public engagement context, working with both a superior's direction and in collaboration with a content writer. A challenge we faced during this project was engaging with our audience during and after the pandemic. Methods we used to stimulate a newly virtual-trained audience were incorporating QR codes and external links to media such as podcasts and video reviews.
