FoodLink is a concept application inspired by the food insecurities during the COVID-19 pandemic of Spring 2020 that allows users of the application to volunteer to deliver groceries to the most at-risk members of their community, such as seniors and those with preexisting health and respiratory conditions such as diabetes and asthma. People from these at-risk demographics can request assistance through the app as well.

The application is only available on Android due to being coded in AppInventor.

Created as part of a partner project with Jack Lieberson, to which the icon is credited.
