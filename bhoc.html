Bernal Heights Outdoor Cinema

Fall 2021. | Brand Identity, Website Redesign, Print Materials.

goal

This project began as a website redesign, but as I looked into it, I quickly realized that a full rebrand was needed by this San Francisco-based film festival nonprofit. By interviewing my supervisors, I investigated the identity of Bernal Heights Outdoor Cinema and created a new visual identity to represent their mission and vision.

stationary

As the final piece to compliment the redesign, I also created letterhead and business cards to support the organization's internal and external communications.

website

The website design was a complete overhaul using a new Wordpress template. Major changes involved the reorganization of main content such as upcoming events, contact information, and sponsorship.
